The Concept

Zerfall is an idea formed by Campbell Jones, the lead developer at Exoplanet Software. Inspired by Treyarch's Call of Duty Zombies franchise, Zerfall's intent was to bring a survival-based 2D zombie platformer to the world.

The Framework

Zerfall runs in Java through a completely custom engine written in Processing. The game is currently in development, and is open to edits from the community on Github.
